ABOUT GRADEGENIUS

GradeGenius is a web application designed to provide grade tracking tools and simplify GPA calculation for students of all levels. We offer a user-friendly interface and support for various grading scales to ensure accurate results.

Founded by students who understand the importance of academic achievement, GradeGenius is dedicated to providing students with the resources they need to succeed.


Achieve academic success with GradeGenius. Our intuitive tools help you set goals, track progress, and understand how your grades impact your GPA.
